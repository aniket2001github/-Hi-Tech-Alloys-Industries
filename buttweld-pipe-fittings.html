Buttweld Pipe Fittings – Premium Quality Products by Hi-Tech Alloys Industries

At Hi-Tech Alloys Industries, we take pride in being one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of Buttweld Pipe Fittings. Our comprehensive range of pipe fittings is widely recognized for its superior quality, durability, and zero defects, making them the preferred choice for various industries.

We manufacture buttweld fittings using premium-quality raw materials, including stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy steel, and more. These fittings are available in various sizes, ranging from 1/4" NB to 4" NB, and can be customized based on our client’s specifications.

Our Product Range

From ¼" NB to 48" NB, our buttweld fittings are available in diverse shapes, sizes, and configurations, including:

  • Elbows (90°, 45°, 180° Long/Short Radius, Reducing Elbows)
  • Tees (Equal, Unequal)
  • Reducers (Concentric, Eccentric)
  • Caps, Crosses, Couplings, Stub Ends, Nipples
  • Custom Fabrications (Swage Nipples, Bosses, Inserts, Adapters)

Material Diversity

  • Stainless Steel: ASTM/ASME SA403 (Grades 304, 316, 321, 347, 904L, etc.)
  • Duplex & Super Duplex Steel: UNS S31803, S32750, S32760
  • Carbon Steel: ASTM/ASME A234 WPB, WPHY 42-70
  • Alloy Steel: WP1, WP5, WP9, WP91
  • Nickel Alloys: Monel 400, Hastelloy C276, Inconel 625, Copper-Nickel (C70600)
  • Custom Alloys: Tailored solutions for unique industrial needs
