Forged Pipe Fittings – High-Performance Solutions by Hi-Tech Alloys Industries

At Hi-Tech Alloys Industries, we are committed to delivering top-quality forged pipe fittings that meet the highest industry standards. As a trusted manufacturer and supplier, we offer a wide range of forged fittings, engineered for superior strength, excellent corrosion resistance, and long-lasting durability. Our products cater to diverse industries, including oil & gas, petrochemical, power plants, pharmaceuticals, and construction.

Our Product Range

Our forged fittings are available in multiple shapes, sizes, and configurations, ensuring seamless integration into various piping systems. Each component undergoes rigorous testing to guarantee flawless performance and reliability.

  • Forged Elbows (45°, 90°)
  • Forged Tees (Equal & Unequal)
  • Forged Cross
  • Couplings & Half Couplings
  • Caps, Hex Nipples, Adapters
  • Socket Weld & Threaded Fittings

Premium-Quality Materials
